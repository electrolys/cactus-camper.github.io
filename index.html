CACTUS CAMPER


Hill country vintage glamping Christian themed campers. The space features two 1970's Shasta boho style vintage campers with a beautiful surrounding garden. We are situated in South Canyon Lake, known for it's crystal blue water lake, convenient to Whitewater Amphitheater, and tubing on the nearby Guadalupe river. You will have access to two unique vintage shasta campers, a beautiful lighted garden tiki area with chimeneas, and a prayer hiking trail with mountain views.
